# FinWell AI

## AI-Powered Financial Wellness & Smart Education Loan Management Platform

FinWell AI is an intelligent financial management platform designed to help students, employees, and education loan applicants make smarter financial decisions using AI-powered insights, budgeting tools, EMI tracking, and predictive financial analytics.

---

# Problem Statement

Many students and employees struggle with:
- Poor budgeting habits
- Missed EMI and bill payments
- Lack of financial awareness
- Difficulty managing loans
- Overspending and debt accumulation

Traditional finance apps only track expenses but fail to provide intelligent financial guidance.

---

# Solution

FinWell AI provides:
- Smart Expense Tracking
- AI-Based Financial Insights
- Budget Management
- EMI Calculation
- Loan Risk Analysis
- Bank Interest Comparison
- Savings Goal Monitoring
- AI Chatbot Assistance
- Outstanding Balance Tracking
